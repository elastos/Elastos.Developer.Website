sendNotification

  • sendNotification(request: NotificationRequest): Promise<void>

  • Sends a in-app notification. Notifications are usually displayed by the launcher/home application, in a notifications panel, and they are directly used to inform users of something they can potentially interact with.

    Parameters

    • request: NotificationRequest

      The notification content.

    Returns Promise<void>

    A promise that can be awaited and catched in case or error.
